We Negotiate When You Say Go

Pick a supplier and tell us you're ready. Our team negotiates the best payment terms and lower MOQ on your behalf — using leverage from AI-prepped market data and our entire client base. You stay in control of the call.

Your Sourcing Team, On the Ground

We're your eyes, ears, and problem-solvers on the factory floor — so you don't have to fly 10,000 miles when issues arise.

Smart Factory Matching

Our AI agents scan factories in and beyond our 50-year network — verifying certifications (OEKO-TEX, ISO, WRAP), pulling real export and delivery history, and scoring on-time rates and defect history. Our team validates every shortlist before it reaches you.

Real-Time Production Tracking

We check in with factories 2x/week. Get photo updates at key milestones and proactive alerts if any issues arise — before they become problems.

Network-Powered Intelligence

Every order improves our factory ratings and pricing data. You benefit from insights across all our clients — know if quotes are fair, which factories deliver, and what to watch for.
